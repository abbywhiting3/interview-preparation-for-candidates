Interview Preparation for Candidates

Practical guidance to help candidates prepare with confidence.

Research the company

Review the employer’s website, services, leadership, and recent news before the interview.

Prepare your examples

Match your experience to the job description and prepare specific stories that show results.

Practice common questions

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why do you want this role?
  • Describe a challenge you solved
  • What are your strengths?

Follow up

Send a thank-you note within 24 hours and reinforce your interest in the role.
